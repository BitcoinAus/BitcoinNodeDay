Bitcoin Node Day

Current node count , (5777 30 May 2015)

Bitcoin node day is an initiative by the Australian Digital Currency Commerce Asocciation ADCCA.org

Why bitcoin node day?

Bitcoin nodes are the back bone of the bitcoin network. Without them the bitcoin network would not survive.

Full nodes perform the following functions on the network.

  1. Filtering transactions and blocks on behalf of lightweight nodes so that lightweight nodes do not need to download every transaction ever made on the network in order to find their own transactions.
  2. Serving historical full blocks to nodes that have been offline for a while. Transmitting new transactions from users to miners.
  3. Broadcasting new blocks from miners to other nodes.

Participate - 30th August 2015

Inspired by Earth Hour, bitcoin node day is designed to raise awarness around bitcoin node decline.

Bitcoin node day will be held on the last Saturday of June each year, starting at Midnight GMT. In 2015, that is the 27th. To particpate, you can simply start bitcoin Core, buy or rent a bitcoin node.

What happens on bitcoin node day?

Put simple, we are asking bitcoin enthusists to install and run a full bitcoin node, such as bitcoin core for 24hrs.

Visit the site or https://getaddr.bitnodes.io and see if the experiment works.

Talk about bitcoin nodes, block sizes and the future of bitcoin with your peers, friends and family.
